Shakuntala of Barrackpore
A sweeping multi-volume historical fiction saga following one woman's extraordinary journey from colonial India to the New World — shaped by faith, hardship, and indomitable will.
Volume I

The Epic

Bihar, British Colonial India

Set in the heart of colonial Bihar, this is the story of Shakuntala — a Hindu woman whose grit is forged by faith and tested by the raw cruelty of British rule. Her journey begins with indentureship recruitment and ends with her boarding the Fatel Rozack, bound for Trinidad.

Trinidad — The New World

About the Author
Lingam

Lingam is the pen name of an author whose roots run deep into the history of the Indo-Caribbean diaspora. His writing draws from the lived memory of indentureship, spiritual resilience, and the long arc of ancestral survival.

"History doesn't repeat — but it breathes. These stories are that breath."

Beyond the page, Lingam is the founder of the Lingram Family Trust — a family of organizations dedicated to community, education, and the elevation of marginalized voices. His work spans publishing, nonprofit advocacy, and platform building, all under the guiding principle of Ahimsa.
